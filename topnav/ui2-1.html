5 Terre

Everyone knows what a tree is, but it is a classification of plants that is not strictly defined. This is because there are many plants that resemble trees, such as shrubs (bush trees), bamboo, and palm trees, but with different ecology. A tree in a broad sense commonly used refers to a plant that grows a certain height above and has a long wooden post with leaves or stems.
